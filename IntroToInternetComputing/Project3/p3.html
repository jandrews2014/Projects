Q: What is Kilometers and Miles?

A: Kilometers a quantitative value for distance in the Metric System.

Miles is a quantitative value for distance in the Standard System.

Q: When was Kilometers and Miles discovered?

A: Kilometers was discovered in 1790 when the French Academy of Sciences developed a new system.

However it was not until 1867 that Kilometer was officially used. The British spelling for Kilometer is

'Kilometre'.

Miles was discovered in 29 B.C. by the Ancient Romans as a way of counting a thousand foot paces.

Q: How is Kilometers and Miles used today?

A: Both Kilometers and Miles are used today to read maps, GPS, and calculating the distance on the road.
