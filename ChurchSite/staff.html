Staffed by the Scalabrini Missionaries

Since 1960, our church has been serving the community and creating a bond founded on our beliefs and our drive to contribute to others. It has always been our mission to create an environment that feels safe and supportive for all of our members and guests. In 2010, we were thrilled to celebrate our 50th Golden Jubilee, and we are excited to continue welcoming new faces every week!

We offer a myriad of ways to worship and serve the community. It is our hope that we can find unique ways for everyone to take part in our church and enjoy themselves in the process. We believe in fun, friendship and support, so visit us soon!

Sacrament Information

Baptism

Parents must be registered, active members of the St. Vincent for six months before they can register for the Baptismal class. The classes are held monthly for parents and god parents, as needed. Baptisms will take place the last Sunday of the same month at 2:30 pm. Please come to the church office with the birth certificate to make arrangements.


Matrimony

Arrangements must be made at least six months in advance. The Catholic party must be a registered, active member of the Parish, using envelopes. Call for an appointment.


Ministry to the Sick

Please call the office if anyone is sick at home, in the hospital, nursing home, or assisted living who wishes to be visited.


Anointing of the Sick

Persons entering the hospital, hospice or who are having surgery are encouraged to be anointed ahead of time. To make an appointment, call the office or see Father after daily Mass.
