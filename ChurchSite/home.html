What We Do

We are a Roman Catholic Church founded in November, 1960 to serve all peoples through worship. We have created a culture of support, devotion, service, instruction and involvement in ways that deepen our beliefs, nurture our spirituality, and bring us closer to God. Check out our services for Mass times, confessions, novenas, praying the rosary and other devotions. Come visit us!
